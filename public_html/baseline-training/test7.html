Instructions

You will hear a sound from the computer. After you hear the sound, a green box will appear on the screen. Touch the green box to hear a second sound. After you hear the second sound, a white box will appear on the screen. If you think the first and second sound go together, touch the white box and say ‘click.’ If the sounds do not go together, then do not touch the box and wait for the box to disappear. During this phase, you get no points or sounds as feedback. Remember, you can read these instructions at any time. Place the instructions in the designated location.
